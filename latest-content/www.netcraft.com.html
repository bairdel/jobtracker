Working at Netcraft

Our talented and dedicated people are the driving force behind our success. We’re successful when you’re successful. That’s why our culture is built on responsibility and respect. This means we focus on making sure you: 

  • Feel welcomed and supported from your first moments at Netcraft

  • Have a genuine voice in shaping what we do and how we do it

  • Are encouraged to explore new ideas and share what you discover

From software engineers to sales executives, accountants to cybercrime analysts, we’re always on the lookout for enthusiastic people who’ll bring new ideas, experiences, and perspectives.

Ally Network

Equality, diversity, and inclusion are very important to us at Netcraft. That’s why we created the Ally Network to support underrepresented groups. This network is part of our company-wide commitment to the inclusion of all colleagues regardless of their gender identity, sexual orientation, race/ethnicity, disability, age, or other specific characteristics — and our aim to create a working environment that is free from bias, harassment, or discrimination.

Mental Health First Aiders

At Netcraft, we recognize the importance of investing in the mental and emotional health of our teammates. Our mental health first aiders are colleagues who have undergone the appropriate training to understand mental health conditions, how to appropriately deal with them, and how to promote a better work environment to support mental health awareness.

Students and Graduates

We welcome many talented computer science and technology students each year at Netcraft, offering summer placements as well as longer-term industrial placements and graduate roles.

Working alongside some of the brightest minds in the industry, you will gain invaluable hands-on experience while applying what you learn to real projects for real customers. We gladly consider applications from first-year students.

Our Hiring Approach

We take hiring seriously at Netcraft — our people are what powers our success. We want our hiring process to be as quick, smooth, and welcoming as possible.

Depending on the role, we’ll usually follow a three-stage hiring process: initial chat, competency-based assessment, and final interview. You’ll get to meet different people along the way. And, there will be plenty of time for you to quiz us as much as you want.

We know that hiring people with different talents, experiences, and perspectives is what enables us to think innovatively. We actively welcome applicants with different backgrounds — and we’re happy to adjust our hiring process if you have any additional needs. Please just let us know.
