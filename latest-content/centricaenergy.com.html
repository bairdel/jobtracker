Trading & Services

Is your business in need of energy or looking to sell energy? Let us help you find the best solution.

PPAs and CPPAs for developers and corporates

With one of Europe’s largest Renewable energy portfolios, we have specialised in Renewable PPAs and Asset Management for more than 15 years. We provide asset owners and investors with Power Purchase Agreements and corporates with Corporate Power Purchase Agreements to support their green ambitions.

Discover our PPA and CPPA sector

Energy Optimisation & Flexibility

Battery energy storage systems play a crucial role in integrating more renewable energy into the grid. Our market-innovating Flexible Energy Solutions help the power grid handle an increase in renewable energy shares as efficiently as possible and secures commercial viability for flexible asset owners.

Learn more about our battery services

Renewable Energy Certificates

We offer certificates with all leading global standardizations and environmental accreditations and tailored and transparent solutions that matches your specific needs. Handling management, trading and retirement of green certificates, we help combine your business’ energy needs with your green ambitions.

Explore our work with certificates

Power & Gas Trading

Our Energy Traders operate in the volatile short-term, day-ahead, and intraday spot markets as well as in forward markets, covering yearly to daily contracts with futures, options, and structured products. Leveraging our extensive quantitative and automated data platform, analytical setup, and market presence across Europe, we capture opportunities and efficiently move energy across grids and borders.

Learn more about our Trading business

Biomethane

Biomethane will play an important role as a key provider of both baseload generation, low-carbon balancing and flexibility as well as an alternative renewable fuel for hard-to-abate industries. Leveraging our extensive experience operating in biomethane markets, we provide long-term green Gas Purchase Agreements (GPAs). Offtaking or supplying green gas and associated certificates, we craft tailored pricing structures and contract durations according to your needs.

Dive into our biomethane offering

LNG

With a presence through the entire value chain, holding liquification buy contracts, ownership of long-term charter ships, re-gas capacity, and financial and physical trading, we’ve built a global LNG business that delivers cargoes almost anywhere in the world. Through our trading operations in both London and Singapore, we’ve transacted hundreds of trades, concluded many charterers, and manage over a hundred active master sales and purchase agreements.

Explore how we work with LNG
