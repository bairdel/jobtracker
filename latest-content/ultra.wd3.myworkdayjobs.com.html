At Ultra Intelligence & Communications, we’re on a journey to provide military personnel with the latest, most customizable solutions and capabilities to enable mission success anywhere in the world. We’re in search of talented individuals who are excited to innovate and help shape the future of the defense technology industry.

Why Ultra I&C?

Our organization offers a fulfilling and rewarding career where innovation thrives. Our team values creativity, embraces collaboration, and empowers individuals to make a difference. At Ultra I&C, your talents will be recognized and you'll have the opportunity to work on cutting-edge projects that shape the future. Start your journey with us and discover endless possibilities. 

Learn more

Working with us

Ignite your career with the Ultra I&C team. Experience unrivaled opportunities for growth, innovation, and impact. By joining our team, you’ll be a part of a dynamic and diverse team that pushes boundaries and drives meaningful change in the world of intelligence and communications. From engineering to corporate functions to front-line production, we offer a variety of roles across the globe. 

Benefits at Ultra I&C

At Ultra I&C, employees are provided with a comprehensive range of employment benefits. We offer competitive salaries that are commensurate with experience and market rates. Additionally, our employees enjoy a generous health and dental insurance package, which covers both routine check-ups and medical emergencies. We also provide vacation and paid time off, allowing our employees the freedom to relax and rejuvenate. We offer retirement plans with a 401k matching program to ensure our employees can have a secure future.

Our ASPIRE Values

Adapt

We forge strong, trusted relationships with our customers and foster meaningful industry partnerships, enabling us to respond with agility to their ever-changing and dynamic environments.

Support

We promote an empowered team and learning environment that encourages the free flow of information, unique perspectives, and new ideas while actively pursuing initiatives that give back to our communities.

Perform

We deliver meticulously tested and proven solutions, ensuring the highest level of performance, problem-solving expertise, and quality deliverables for our customers, partners, and each other.

Innovate

We cultivate a relentless spirit of innovation and deliver on our promises by developing and implementing the most cutting-edge approaches.

Respect

We foster a culture of kindness and equality by valuing our work, celebrating our successes, prioritizing individual well-being, and ensuring fair treatment of all employees, partners, and customers.

Exceed

We conduct business with unwavering ethics, prioritizing safety, honesty, diversity, communication, and inclusion while consistently surpassing customer expectations with uncompromising quality.
