Careers

 

Becrypt is a fun, friendly, cutting edge cybersecurity company based in central London. As we continue to develop and deliver pioneering cybersecurity solutions to Government and industry, and as our business continues to grow, we are always looking for new talent to expand the Becrypt team.

With a positive, diverse and inclusive atmosphere, we focus continuously on promoting equality for the benefit of our employees, products and community.

We are an SME with over half of our workforce in technical roles. Our developers are exceptional in their field, working on the latest technologies in a fast moving and innovative environment. Our office is casual but delivery focussed; we use an agile methodology with regular stand-ups and sprints (virtual where required).

Our working environment is flexible with a minimum of one to two days in the office each week.

Pension

Medical

Flexible Working

Holiday

25 days (+ UK bank holidays), increasing to 27 days after two years, 28 days after five years, and 30 days after ten years of service with an option to purchase up to 5 additional days holidays.

Ride To Work Scheme

Regular Social Events

Fun social activities - to name a few - crazy golf, darts, escape rooms, pub quizzes and many more.
