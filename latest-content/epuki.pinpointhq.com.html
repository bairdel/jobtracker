What to expect during your application

Our application process is designed to be simple and straightforward. Simply submit a CV that highlights your relevant work experience and skills, and what you would bring to the role.

We assess all applications on merit and will respond to your application as quickly as possible. It’s our promise to update all applicants on the outcome of their application, good news or bad, so remember to keep an eye on your emails. 

We utilise a range of assessment materials as well as interviews / discussions.  If you are successful in reaching that stage of the process, one of our HR colleagues will guide you through what to expect and provide details of who you’ll be meeting. 

An interview with EPUKI can often be described as conversational; we prefer an open and honest two-way discussion. This is an opportunity for us to get to know you and the value you can add to our business, as well as a time for you to ask questions and understand more about working with us.  

We look for people who are curious, flexible and collaborative. EPUKI is a great place for people who want a rewarding career that contributes to the UK and Ireland’s low carbon energy journey.

Once you've applied, don't forget to add epuki@pinpoint.email to your safe senders list so you don't miss out on important updates from us.
