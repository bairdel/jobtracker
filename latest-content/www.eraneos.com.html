Your future workplace

Eraneos is an independent, international consultancy firm for strategy development, IT and digitalisation. From our offices in London, our experts are shaping a new digital era and helping our clients with the most complex organisational and digital challenges. We strive to build a place where bright minds have the ambition to challenge the status quo. We want to create the right environment where everyone can fully develop their potential. We work together. We learn together. And we succeed together.

General Application Process

Apply

Apply easily and fast by sending us an email.

Screening

We will screen your application documents and will get back to you as soon as possible.

Video Call

First step is an introduction interview to get to know each other.

Interview Day

Our interview day consists of three separate interviews. You will receive a case 24 hours prior to the first interview which fits to your profile. Please prepare the case and present it as part of the second interview.

Offer

If we believe we are a match, you will receive our offer within one business week.

Our Benefits

Grow with Eraneos

We’re not only a “Great Place to Work” certified company, but we are also a “great place to grow.” We provide a platform for personal and professional growth, which is built upon our people and culture initiatives. These are focused around our core values: Dedication, Together, and Impact.
Discover how Eraneos can support you in your future development.

Personal Growth

You are in control of your development. We don’t believe that “one size fits all” and cater for individual career paths fitting your ambitions and interests. Through our Eraneos network we offer almost endless different opportunities to develop and grow – and we support you in achieving your goals.

Collaborative workplace culture

We are successful when we join forces. Our working atmosphere is characterised by the exchange of ideas, feedback, and thoughts as well as mutual support. It is important to us that everyone in our organisation’s network is heard and respected, regardless of their role or position in the company. We build teams based on capabilities, not hierarchies.

Genuine Leadership

We help our colleagues grow because we truly care for them. We act honestly and ethically within an environment where everyone has the opportunity to take on leadership responsibilities. Excellence is the result of our belief in the value of different opinions, expertise, and diversity. Leadership comes from the collective.

Fringe Benefits

We believe in providing holistic rewards, covering everything from continuous learning to wellbeing, ensuring a rewarding work experience for you. That’s why we offer an extensive benefits package, including health and social initiatives, and many more. We’re dedicated to empowering our employees in every facet of their lives, both within and beyond the workplace.
