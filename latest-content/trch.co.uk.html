    Marketing Officer

    Contract Type: Fixed term for 12 months
    Working Hours: 25 hours per week, Monday to Friday
    Worker Type: Hybrid
    Salary: £24,572.34 (this is pro rata due to the part time nature)
    Location: Theatre Royal & Royal Concert Hall, Nottingham, NG1 5ND

    We’re looking for a dynamic and creative marketing professional. You will be responsible for undertaking allocated marketing campaigns for TRCH by writing, managing, developing, implementing and evaluating campaign plans. You will be required to maximise high levels of income along with developing new audiences from a diverse community throughout the City of Nottingham and beyond and to raise the profile of the venue.

    The ideal candidate will have:

    • Knowledge and experience of both an income and customer-focused approach to the marketing of shows and events undertaken in an entertainment venue.
    • A thorough knowledge of marketing practices and experience of using all elements of the marketing mix, including above and below the line activity, PR, Communications, Database Marketing, Market Research, monitoring and evaluation.
    • Experience in successfully implementing marketing activities to increase target markets and develop new audiences.
    • Experience of working under pressure, prioritising projects and work to meet tight deadlines, and multi-tasking as appropriate.
    • Experience in using digital and social media channels to promote and advertise shows and events.
    • Experience of liaising with promoters and producers as well as dealing with press and media contacts.
